Cookie Policy

Information about how we use cookies and similar technologies on VEDARA.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential
Session Cookies
 Required for basic site functionality, user authentication, and security features. These cannot be disabled. Session or up to 24 hours
Analytics
Performance Cookies
 Collect anonymous information about how visitors use our platform to help us improve user experience. Up to 2 years
Functional
Preference Cookies
 Remember your settings and preferences, such as language preference or regional settings. Up to 1 year

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you, such as logging in or filling in forms.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us know which pages are popular and how visitors move around the site.

Managing Cookies

Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete certain cookies. However, disabling essential cookies may affect how our website functions.

Cookie Controls by Browser

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Third-Party Cookies

Service Providers

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies to provide features like analytics, maps, or weather data. These providers have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

Analytics Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our platform. This service uses cookies to collect information about your use of VEDARA.

Cookie Preferences & Questions

For questions about our cookie policy or to manage your cookie preferences, contact us at privacy@vedara.com.

Cookie Transparency

We believe in being transparent about our use of cookies and similar technologies. This policy explains how and why we use these technologies on VEDARA.

User Control

We respect your right to control cookies. While essential cookies cannot be disabled without affecting functionality, you can control other cookie types through your browser settings.

Policy Updates
