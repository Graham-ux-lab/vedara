Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

Name, email address, phone number, and farm location details when you create an account.

Farm Data

Crop information, planting dates, harvest data, soil conditions, and irrigation details you provide.

Usage Data

How you interact with our platform, features used, and device information for service improvement.

Data Type Purpose Retention
Account Information Service delivery and communication While account active + 90 days
Farm Operations Data Agricultural recommendations and analytics While account active
Usage Analytics Service improvement and feature development 24 months anonymized

How We Use Your Data

Service Delivery

To provide personalized farming recommendations, weather alerts, and crop management tools.

Communication

To send important updates, service notifications, and agricultural insights relevant to your farm.

Improvement

To analyze aggregated, anonymized data for platform enhancement and agricultural research.

Data Protection

Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS.

Access Control

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your personal information.

Regular Audits

Our security practices undergo regular third-party audits to maintain compliance with industry standards.

Your Rights

Access and Correction

You can access and update your personal information at any time through your account settings.

Data Export

Request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format for transfer to another service.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Data Responsibility
