Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees or long-term contracts.

Do you offer discounts for NGOs or educational institutions?

Yes! We offer special pricing for non-profits, educational institutions, and research organizations. Contact our sales team for details.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is yours. We'll keep your data for 90 days after cancellation, and you can export it anytime during that period.
