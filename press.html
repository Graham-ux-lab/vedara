VEDARA

Transforming agriculture through innovative technology solutions. We build tools that empower farmers and promote sustainable farming practices across Africa.

Our mission is to make advanced farming technology accessible to every farmer, regardless of farm size or location.

Company Overview

VEDARA is a leading agritech company based in Nairobi, Kenya. Founded in 2018, we serve over 100,000 farmers across 15 African countries.

We specialize in AI-powered farming solutions, IoT sensor networks, and digital marketplace platforms that connect farmers to markets.
