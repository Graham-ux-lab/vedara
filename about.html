Our Story

Founded by a team of agronomists, software engineers, and farmers, VEDARA was born from a simple observation: smallholder farmers were using 20th-century tools to solve 21st-century challenges.

VEDARA was founded in Nairobi, Kenya, after a year of research with 200+ smallholder farmers. Our first product was a simple SMS-based crop advisory service.

Launched our first mobile app, reaching 5,000 farmers across East Africa. Partnered with agricultural research institutions to improve our crop database.

Expanded to West Africa. Introduced IoT sensor integration for soil moisture and weather monitoring. Reached 20,000 active users.

Launched marketplace features connecting farmers directly to buyers. Introduced AI-powered disease detection using image recognition.

Expanded to Southern Africa. Partnered with major agricultural banks for integrated financing solutions. Reached 50,000 farmers.

Launched API platform for developers and agribusinesses. Introduced carbon credit tracking for sustainable farming practices.

Currently serving over 100,000 farmers across 15 African countries. Continuously innovating to make farming smarter and more sustainable.
