Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within 5 minutes.

Can I use VEDARA on multiple devices?

Yes, your VEDARA account can be accessed on multiple devices simultaneously. You can use our web platform, iOS app, and Android app all with the same account.

How do I add a new farm to my account?

Navigate to "My Farms" in your dashboard, click "Add New Farm," and fill in the farm details including location, size, and soil type. You can add multiple farms to one account.

What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

Your data is preserved for 90 days after cancellation. During this period, you can export your data. After 90 days, data is anonymized and may be permanently deleted.

How accurate are the weather predictions?

Our weather data comes from multiple sources and has 85-90% accuracy for 7-day forecasts. We provide hyper-local forecasts based on your farm's GPS coordinates.

Can I use VEDARA offline?

Our mobile app has offline capabilities for data entry and viewing cached information. Sync occurs automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

VEDARA Support

We are committed to providing excellent support to all our farmers and users. Our help center is designed to provide quick answers and comprehensive guidance for using our platform effectively.

Our support team consists of agricultural experts and technical specialists who understand both farming and technology.

Support Hours

Live Chat: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM East Africa Time.

Phone Support: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM East Africa Time.

Email Support: 24/7 with responses within 24 hours during business days.

Emergency issues receive priority attention regardless of time.

We believe in empowering farmers with knowledge. Our help resources are designed to educate as well as solve problems.
